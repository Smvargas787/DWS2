Serivces

"Lifting Up" is a women ministry group located in Ocoee, Florida. Grace Bible Church has been the home of this ministry since 2005 and has only continued to group. This group was made for women to go somewhere where they could rebuild their strength in their faith as well as find some women to confide in when times get tough. The women in the group talk to each other in hopes of being supported by them and being spiritual healed by speaking what's on their mind.
